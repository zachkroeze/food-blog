r is for:

Ramen

Ingredients

500-600 g

Pork loin

2 pkgs

Ramen noodles

4

Eggs

1 handful

Fresh cilantro

1 sheet

Nori

2

Green onions

3/4 cup

Miso

1 tbsp

Vegetable oil

2 tbsp

Soy sauce

1 tbsp

Rice wine vinegar

Directions

Preaheat oven to 425F. Combine 1/4 cup of miso, 1 tbsp oil, and 1 tbsp soy sauce in a boil. Rub glaze on the pork loin. Place in oven for 35-40 min. Let rest for 10min.

Place eggs in a pot, and fill with enough cold water to cover eggs plus an extra inch. Bring to a boil. Take off heat and cover for 6-8 minutes, depending on how runny you want the eggs.

In a pot combine 1.5L of water, 1/2 cup of miso, 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar, and 1 tbsp of soy sauce. Bring to a boil.

Place ramen into boiling broth and cook according to package directions. Remove with a small strainer or slotted spoon. Place into large bowls.

Wash bean sprouts, cut nori into smaller strips, chop cilantro and green onions.

Cover noodles with broth, and add eggs and topings.
